Our Values


“ I am still not sure where I stand in the lives of you students. Most of you treat me as their friend, a companion to talk to, a daily Hi-Bye person or someone who you come up to whenever you are hungry. Well, the last one makes more sense and that makes me feel special and different.

It’s been more than 25 years this canteen has been running now. I don’t really like to talk much about me and I am a very later addition to it but the family I have become to this college and it’s students makes me fall short of words. I live in Vikhroli with my family; we are grateful and content with what we have. I wouldn’t resist to say this and I definitely think that the canteen is the most chaotic and packed place in this college. And in return, this chaos and hustle-bustle is what makes it the most lively place in the college. Not a day goes by when I am tired of the work or want to take rest. It is rather the most fun job. All of us reach here by 7-8 in the morning to prepare the meals for the day and then as the students start rolling in, the canteen comes to life! Not one person working in this campus would deny this that the things Somaiya Trust has done for us all is par excellence, giving daily livelihood to the hundreds and thousands working here in all their small small sectors throughout the campus. This college is the breeding ground of the generation of students who will go on to assume highest of positions in the biggest of companies around. Also, it is a plus-point for our business. (smirks)

Kaafi baatein ho gayi. Cutting loge? ”

- Jayant Vasu Shetty, popularly famed by the name of, ‘Anna of the Canteen’.
